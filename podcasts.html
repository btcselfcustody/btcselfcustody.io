Bitcoin Podcasts

Below is a list of must-listen podcasts about Bitcoin and the disgrace of fiat money.

The "What is Money?" Show"

The What is Money? Show (WiM) is a top philosophy podcast exploring wisdom, intelligence, and meaning. By examining history through the lens of money, it reveals the power of language and the impact of monetary systems on civilization.

Simply Bitcoin

A daily Bitcoin show that keeps you informed about the peaceful Bitcoin revolution. Hosted by Nico and Opti, the show dives into the latest developments in the Bitcoin space, while Rustin and Dante provide in-depth coverage of the most important news and events shaping the industry.

The Bitcoin Standard Podcast

Saifedean's The Bitcoin Standard Podcast explores Bitcoin and Austrian economics. Join the weekly saifedean.com seminar, where global learners discuss online courses, economics, politics, and more, sometimes with special guests.

Coin Stories

Learn about the future of money and how Bitcoin proposes a solution to the problems in our monetary system. Why is wealth concentration growing? Will the U.S. dollar always be the world reserve currency? Is Bitcoin the "internet of money?"
